Membership Levels

NP Membership

For non-profit organizations. Benefits include access to networking events, member directory listing, and marketing support.

Bronze Membership

Basic membership for small businesses. Includes all NP benefits plus a business profile on the Chamber website and eligibility to participate in Chamber events.

Silver Membership

Ideal for growing businesses. Includes all Bronze benefits plus enhanced directory listing, additional marketing opportunities, and discounts on event sponsorships.

Gold Membership

Premium membership for established businesses. Includes all Silver benefits plus priority event sponsorships, exclusive networking opportunities, and dedicated support from Chamber staff.
