Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name: Raymore Chamber of Commerce

Raymore is the town in which I live which is why I picked it.

Site Purpose

  • Offer business owners & community members a way to connect with each other
  • Offer an area for members of the Chamber of Commerce to display products & services
  • Display upcoming business events of interest

Scenarios

  1. Events (calendar?): Hiring Event, Professional Development Class, Raymore Chamber of Commerce Board Meeting, Ribbon cutting for new business grand opening, Golf Tournament Fundraiser
  2. Contacts: Page specifically for request to contact board of directors/requests to join the chamber of commerce & main contact phone number & email for the chamber of commerce in the footer of every page
  3. Sponsored businesses that are part of the Chamber of Commerce

Texts & Background? - #EEFBFB

Typography
